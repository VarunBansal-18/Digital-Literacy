The "Receive Money" UPI QR Code Scam

A common cybercrime targeting college students is the UPI payment request or QR code scam. In this scenario, a student lists an item for sale online, and a scammer quickly contacts them posing as an eager buyer to build false trust. The scammer insists on paying via a UPI app but, instead of transferring the funds, sends a "Collect Request" or a customized QR code to the student. The scammer then instructs the student to scan the code and enter their UPI PIN, falsely claiming that this step is necessary to receive the payment. Once the student enters their PIN, the agreed-upon amount is instantly deducted from their own bank account and transferred to the scammer. This type of fraud frequently targets young adults who might be distracted or rushing to complete a transaction, resulting in sudden financial loss, significant stress, and an inability to pay for daily college expenses.
